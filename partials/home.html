Kolorize

A color palette creation and HTML preview tool.
Get Started


palette

Create Color Palettes

Use a color picker or extract colors from images easily and add them to your palette. Browse other users palettes and if you see one you like, you can save a copy and modify it yourself.

search

Search Flickr

Feeling inspired by something but don't want to hunt to find images? Use our search and scroll through the results. If you see something you like, simply click the image and a palette of colors will be extracted from it.

laptop

Preview Web/Print Media

See how your palette would look on a webpage, flyer, greeting card, or poster. Multiple preview images will be created so you don't have to manually swap out colors.
